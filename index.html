My Mission:

Two of my biggest passions and interests since childhood is coding and playing video games. I have always been curious on how computers and video games work. However, I was not able to really learn about technology as my opportunities throughout elementary and middle school was extremely limited. As I was entering high school, more and more opportunities became available to me in order to learn more about computer science. My high school has a variety of computer science classes and I was motivated to take as many as I can. Even though the class made projects and practiced coding, I was not satisfied with the limited amount of projects we were making. I wanted to apply my knowledge to a project more interactive and meaningful to both myself and the world. As such, I took it upon myself to teach myself the programming languages I was not taught yet such as HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I have been building my own projects I thought were useful in gaining experience. My experience includes making websites and web applications, discord bots, and designing projects for other people to build. I am the kind of person who does not like to wait to be taught and likes to be challenged.
